Welcome to Interview Shorts - National Institute of Technology Raipur.

We are a community-driven platform dedicated to helping National Institute of Technology Raipur students share their on-campus and off-campus interview experiences. Our goal is to provide valuable insights, tips, and tricks that can assist you in preparing for your future interviews and online assessments (OAs).

At Interview Shorts - National Institute of Technology Raipur, you can find a wide range of interview experiences shared by your fellow students. These experiences include details about the interview process, the questions asked, and other useful information. Additionally, you can explore tips and tricks provided by experienced students who have successfully navigated through interviews and OAs.

We encourage active participation from all National Institute of Technology Raipur students. By sharing your interview experiences and tips, you can contribute to the community and help your peers in their interview preparation journey. Together, we can create a valuable resource that empowers National Institute of Technology Raipur students to excel in their interviews and secure their dream careers.

If you have any questions, feedback, or suggestions, please feel free to contact us. We are constantly working to improve the platform and make it more helpful for all users.
